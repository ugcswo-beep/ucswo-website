About UCSWO

The Ugandan Community of Southwestern Ontario (UCSWO) is a nonprofit organization of people of Ugandan descent in Southwest Ontario, Canada.

Our Vision

To unite and empower Ugandans in Southwestern Ontario, fostering inclusivity, cultural pride, and economic growth while nurturing a strong sense of community.

Our Mission

To foster unity among Ugandans, promote cultural heritage, support newcomers, and advance social cohesion, economic prosperity, and empowerment while contributing positively to the wider Southwestern Ontario community.
